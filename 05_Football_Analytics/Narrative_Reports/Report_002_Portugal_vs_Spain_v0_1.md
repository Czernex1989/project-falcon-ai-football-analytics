# 4. Spain Analysis

Wide players tried to stretch Portugal's defence.

The team stayed calm despite the lack of goals.

Patience was rewarded in the final minutes.

### Portugal

The team tried to play forward quickly.

### Spain

Players changed positions to create space.

# 6. Defensive Phase

### Spain
